# Code of Conduct

vibly-chain contributors are expected to keep project spaces respectful,
constructive, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about technical risks.
- Keep criticism specific to code, design, documentation, or process.
- Respect maintainer decisions about scope, timing, and safety.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private security details without maintainer coordination.
- Sharing private contact information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation
when behavior harms the project or contributors.
